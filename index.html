VERITAS VARSITY VERITAS HOSTS CARDINAL ONAYEKAN, BISHOP PAPROCKI, FIVE OTHERS.
The Veritas University on Wednesday 10 January, 2024 hosted Cardinal Onayekan and his longtime friend, Bishop Thomas John Paprocki of the Catholic Diocese of Springfield, United States of America and five others as part of efforts by the Cardinal to solicit support for the University in areas of funding and academic cooperation.

AfDB ADOPTS VERITAS AS CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE FOR COMPUTER CODING.
The President of the African Development Bank (AfDB), Dr. Akinwumi Adesina, has announced that the Bank has adopted the Veritas University of Nigeria as it's Centre of Excellence for Computer Coding for Employment in Nigeria. Read More

VERITAS UNIVERSITY GETS TIMES HIGHER EDUCATION RANKING
Veritas University participated for the first time in the 2023 Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Rankings on Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It was a First Great Step. The University was ranked among 11 Universities in Nigeria. On Webometric Rankings, Veritas University got the “Reporter Status” For the 2023 World University Rankings
